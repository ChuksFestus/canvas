Canvas comes packed with features, yet keeps a light footprint on your system.

  • edit

    Markdown

    Like writing with markdown shorthand syntax? You'll love the split-screen editor with built-in keyboard shortcuts.

  • invert_colors

    Theming

    Blank Sass files are provided so you can create your own child theme and give your blog a personal touch.

  • date_range

    Scheduled Posts

    Draft posts and schedule them to publish exactly on the date and time you choose, or save your work for later.

  • comment

    Comments

    Disqus comments can easily be turned on or off. Engage your readers and followers with this powerful tool.

  • trending_up

    Analytics

    Native Google Analytics integration support. Just drop in your tracking id and start building reports today.

  • code

    Open Source

    Everything here is open source, so you can theme, extend and integrate to your heart’s content. Developers welcome.

Write faster and better than you ever have before with Canvas. Give your readers a delightfully minimal experience to keep coming back to.

Integrate with GitHub, Twitter and Facebook to take advantage of everything Canvas has to offer with its dynamic social profile features.

The split-screen editor, with Markdown syntax on the left and a live preview on the right, lets you type and format your writing on-the-fly.

Canvas is a place to call home. With its crisp and beautiful UI/UX, you'll have a reason to fall in love with blogging time and time again.

About

Canvas is a simple, powerful blog publishing platform that lets you to share your stories with the world. Its beautifully designed interface and completely customizable framework allows you to write and publish your own blog, giving you the tools to make it easy and even fun to do.
